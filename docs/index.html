Internet of Things for Entrepreneurs

This is a course with video lectures and other documents teaching how to make a connected object and why "IoT" (Internet of Things) is such a big deal today for entrepreneurs, industries and consumers.

Make an object

We will build an object that shows on screen the air quality (the level of pollution) in your city, in real time. You will need about 35$ to buy the material for this object, and access to a soldering equipment.

Getting the broader picture

After creating an object, you will learn through video lectures why connected objects are such a big deal today: IoT is projected to be https://www.forbes.com/sites/louiscolumbus/2017/01/29/internet-of-things-market-to-reach-267b-by-2020/#5a988f42609b[a 267 billion dollars market] by 2020!
